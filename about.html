I'm Sabrina, the cake designer!

 

 

 I was born in Belgium and moved with my family to Ecuador at the age of 2.

 

When I was a kid I used to bake with my mother the typical pastries from Belgium. Mmmmmm there were delicious!

 

The design essence was always inside of me since I used to go to with my father to his fabric factory.

After I got my kids, I started to bake simples cakes and cookies for them.

 

When my oldest boy was 8 I decided to go to "WILTON" the big decoration company for cakes. It was like a dream come true. In that place I realized that I wanted to be a Cake Designer!

 

I'm always taking classes here and there about backing, decoration and design

 

THIS IS MY REAL PASSION!
